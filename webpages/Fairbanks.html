Bokeh Plot

This is the monthly production figure. The x axis is month and y axis is the production of each month. We take the best performance data and median performance data to show. TMY2 and TMY3 lines are the prediction values from PVWatts which is a system they can collect solar source data and predict solar panel production. As you see, the production in the summer is pretty high because the sun exposes on the sky all the day, but the situation in the winter is opposite.
